How to build my group?
Just go to "group" and select [create a group] or choose an existed group. You will be added into a group if your application has been approved by the manager.
How to build my project?
If you didn't join a group or want to create a project for new group, please create your gourp firstly. Then you could click the group name and select [Create a project].

How to contact my group member?
You could send timer messages to your group memebr. Please find the name in the group and click the name!
How to set up notification?
You could choose different notification method including SMS, Emails and timer notifications. You could also mute notification in Setting on your left. :)
